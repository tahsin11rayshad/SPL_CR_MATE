CR Mate Advantages

Seamless Notice Sharing

CR Mate offers a streamlined approach to notice sharing. Each type of message, whether it's an academic notice, club announcement, classroom code, or study material, gets its own individual page. CRs can simply fill out a dedicated form for each notice type, and with a click of a button, the notice is uploaded to the Notice Board page.

Exclusive CR Authority

At CR Mate, we understand the pivotal role of CRs in disseminating information effectively. That's why CRs have exclusive authority to publish and manage notices on the platform. This ensures that only relevant and accurate information is shared with their classmates, enhancing communication within the university community.

Unique University Code

To maintain the privacy and exclusivity of the notice board, each registered CR is provided with a Unique University Code (UUC). The CRs will share this UUC solely with their fellow classmates. As a result, only students with a valid UUC can access the notices uploaded by their respective CRs. This feature ensures that no one outside their university can view the notices, adding an additional layer of security and confidentiality.
